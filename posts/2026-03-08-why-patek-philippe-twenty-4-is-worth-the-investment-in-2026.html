Patek Philippe Twenty-4: Overview

The Twenty-4 by Patek Philippe represents the pinnacle of watchmaking. In-house calibers create a timepiece meant to outlast generations.

How to Authenticate Patek Philippe Twenty-4

The Patek Philippe Twenty-4 is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: 18K gold stamp must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic Twenty-4 uses quality stones with precise setting. Check for gaps in the bezel setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests hollow construction.
