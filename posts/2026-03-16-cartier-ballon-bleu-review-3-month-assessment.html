Cartier Ballon Bleu: Overview

The Ballon Bleu from Cartier represents over a century of expertise. 18K gold and meticulous finishing make this a true collectible.

How to Authenticate Cartier Ballon Bleu

The Cartier Ballon Bleu is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: serial engraving must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic Ballon Bleu uses quality stones with precise setting. Check for gaps in the prong setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests base metal.
