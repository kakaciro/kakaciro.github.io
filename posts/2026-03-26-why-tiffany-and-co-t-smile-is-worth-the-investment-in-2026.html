Tiffany and Co T Smile: Overview

The T Smile from Tiffany and Co represents 184 years of heritage. 18K gold and precise stone selection create truly iconic pieces.

How to Authenticate Tiffany and Co T Smile

The Tiffany and Co T Smile is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: platinum 950 mark must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic T Smile uses natural gemstones with precise setting. Check for gaps in the bezel setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests hollow construction.

Jewelry Care for Tiffany and Co T Smile

Store in the original box when not wearing. Apply perfume and lotions first to preserve gold luster and stone brilliance.

Clean monthly using a soft brush and mild soap and water. Avoid ultrasonic cleaners as they may damage delicate stones.

Have prongs and settings checked annually to prevent stone loss. Proper storage will keep your T Smile sparkling for decades.
