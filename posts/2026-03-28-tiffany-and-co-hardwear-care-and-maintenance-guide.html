Tiffany and Co HardWear Care and Maintenance Guide

2026-03-28

Tiffany and Co HardWear: Overview

The HardWear from Tiffany and Co represents 184 years of heritage. 18K gold and precise stone selection create truly iconic pieces.

How to Authenticate Tiffany and Co HardWear

The Tiffany and Co HardWear is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: VCA hallmark must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic HardWear uses natural gemstones with precise setting. Check for gaps in the claw setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests gold plating.

Jewelry Care for Tiffany and Co HardWear

Store in the original box when not wearing. Apply perfume and lotions first to preserve gold luster and stone brilliance.

Clean monthly using a soft brush and mild soap and water. Avoid ultrasonic cleaners as they may damage delicate stones.

Have prongs and settings checked annually to prevent stone loss. Proper storage will keep your HardWear sparkling for decades.
