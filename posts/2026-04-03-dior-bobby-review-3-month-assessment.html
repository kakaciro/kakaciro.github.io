Dior Bobby: Overview

The Bobby from Maison Dior showcases Gallic elegance. Meticulous embroidery and hand-finished details define its aesthetic.

How to Authenticate Dior Bobby

Authenticating a Dior Bobby requires examining key details that distinguish originals from replicas:

Key Points: check the craftsmanship, check the material, check the hardware, check the stitching.

Hardware on an authentic Bobby shows precise engravings with clean edges. Counterfeits often display inconsistent spacing. Examine the blind stamp under good lighting.

The handle flexibility is consistent throughout. Authentic pieces use full-grain leather that develops natural character over time.

Dior Bobby Price Guide

The Dior Bobby occupies a distinct position in the luxury bag market:

Retail: USD 2000 to USD 15000, varying by size and special order.

Secondary Market: Pre-owned examples typically sell for 100-140% of retail depending on completeness. Rare discontinued sizes can exceed retail significantly.

Price Factors: Togo leather, gold hardware and special color affect final price.

Care and Maintenance for Dior Bobby

Store your Bobby upright in the dust bag when not in use. Avoid exposure to direct sunlight to preserve the leather suppleness.

Clean with a soft dry cloth monthly. Condition leather every 3-6 months using only approved products to maintain the natural patina.

For hardware oxidation consult a professional. Annual professional cleaning will ensure your Bobby lasts for generations.
