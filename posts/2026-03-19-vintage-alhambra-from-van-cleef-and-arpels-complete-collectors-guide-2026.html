Van Cleef and Arpels Vintage Alhambra: Overview

The Vintage Alhambra by Van Cleef and Arpels captures romantic elegance. Alhambra motifs are hand-set by master artisans using 18K gold.

How to Authenticate Van Cleef and Arpels Vintage Alhambra

The Van Cleef and Arpels Vintage Alhambra is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: VCA hallmark must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic Vintage Alhambra uses VVS diamonds with precise setting. Check for gaps in the bezel setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests gold plating.

Van Cleef and Arpels Vintage Alhambra Price Guide

Jewelry pricing depends on collection year:

Retail: USD 2000 to USD 15000, varying by material and collection.

Secondary Market: estate pieces typically retains 60-80% of retail. limited editions command premiums.

Jewelry Care for Van Cleef and Arpels Vintage Alhambra

Store in the original box when not wearing. Apply perfume and lotions first to preserve gold luster and stone brilliance.

Clean monthly using a soft brush and mild soap and water. Avoid ultrasonic cleaners as they may damage delicate stones.

Have prongs and settings checked annually to prevent stone loss. Proper storage will keep your Vintage Alhambra sparkling for decades.
