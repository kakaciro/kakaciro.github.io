Louis Vuitton Petite Malle: Overview

The Petite Malle is a versatile house icon from Louis Vuitton. Monogram canvas construction ensures durability for daily use.

How to Authenticate Louis Vuitton Petite Malle

Authenticating a Louis Vuitton Petite Malle requires examining key details that distinguish originals from replicas:

Key Points: check the craftsmanship, check the material, check the hardware, check the stitching.

Hardware on an authentic Petite Malle shows precise engravings with clean edges. Counterfeits often display inconsistent spacing. Examine the blind stamp under good lighting.

The handle flexibility is consistent throughout. Authentic pieces use premium canvas that develops natural character over time.

Louis Vuitton Petite Malle Price Guide

The Louis Vuitton Petite Malle occupies a distinct position in the luxury bag market:

Retail: USD 2000 to USD 15000, varying by hardware finish and special order.

Secondary Market: Pre-owned examples typically sell for 80-120% of retail depending on completeness. Rare discontinued sizes can exceed retail significantly.

Price Factors: Exotic skin, rose gold and rare hardware affect final price.
