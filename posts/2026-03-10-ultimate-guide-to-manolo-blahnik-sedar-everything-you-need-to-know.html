Manolo Blahnik Sedar: Overview

The Sedar from Manolo Blahnik represents the pinnacle of shoemaking art. Hand-crafted in Italy with premium leathers, it delivers timeless style.

How to Authenticate Manolo Blahnik Sedar

Authenticating a Manolo Blahnik Sedar requires examining key details that distinguish originals from replicas:

Key Points: check the craftsmanship, check the material, check the hardware, check the stitching.

Hardware on an authentic Sedar shows precise engravings with clean edges. Counterfeits often display inconsistent spacing. Examine the date code under good lighting.

The handle flexibility is consistent throughout. Authentic pieces use hand-stitched linen that develops natural character over time.

Manolo Blahnik Sedar Price Guide

The Manolo Blahnik Sedar occupies a distinct position in the luxury bag market:

Retail: USD 2000 to USD 15000, varying by size and region.

Secondary Market: Pre-owned examples typically sell for 100-140% of retail depending on completeness. Rare limited editions can exceed retail significantly.

Price Factors: Exotic skin, gold hardware and special color affect final price.
