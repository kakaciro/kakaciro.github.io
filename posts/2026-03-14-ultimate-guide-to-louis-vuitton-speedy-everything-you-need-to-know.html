The Louis Vuitton Speedy occupies a distinct position in the luxury bag market:

Secondary Market: Pre-owned examples typically sell for 80-120% of retail depending on completeness. Rare limited editions can exceed retail significantly.

Price Factors: Togo leather, gold hardware and custom stitching affect final price.
