Care and Maintenance for Manolo Blahnik Mermaid

Store your Mermaid upright in the dust bag when not in use. Avoid exposure to direct sunlight to preserve the leather suppleness.

Clean with a soft dry cloth monthly. Condition leather every 3-6 months using only approved products to maintain the natural patina.

For hardware oxidation consult a professional. Annual professional cleaning will ensure your Mermaid lasts for generations.
