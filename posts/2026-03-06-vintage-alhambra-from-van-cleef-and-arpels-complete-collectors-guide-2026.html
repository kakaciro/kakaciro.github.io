Van Cleef and Arpels Vintage Alhambra: Overview

The Vintage Alhambra by Van Cleef and Arpels captures romantic elegance. Alhambra motifs are hand-set by master artisans using 18K gold.

How to Authenticate Van Cleef and Arpels Vintage Alhambra

The Van Cleef and Arpels Vintage Alhambra is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: 18K gold stamp must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic Vintage Alhambra uses mother of pearl with precise setting. Check for gaps in the bezel setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests gold plating.
