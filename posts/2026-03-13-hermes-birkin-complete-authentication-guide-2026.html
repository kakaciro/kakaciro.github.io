Hermes Birkin: Overview

The Birkin is a legendary icon from the Parisian house. Craftsmen dedicate 18-24 hours of meticulous hand-stitching using premium leather.

Care and Maintenance for Hermes Birkin

Store your Birkin upright in the dust bag when not in use. Avoid exposure to direct sunlight to preserve the leather suppleness.

Clean with a soft dry cloth monthly. Condition leather every 3-6 months using only approved products to maintain the natural patina.

For hardware oxidation consult a professional. Annual professional cleaning will ensure your Birkin lasts for generations.
