Dior Lady Dior Price Guide

The Dior Lady Dior occupies a distinct position in the luxury bag market:

Care and Maintenance for Dior Lady Dior

Store your Lady Dior upright in the dust bag when not in use. Avoid exposure to direct sunlight to preserve the leather suppleness.

Clean with a soft dry cloth monthly. Condition leather every 3-6 months using only approved products to maintain the natural patina.

For hardware oxidation consult a professional. Annual professional cleaning will ensure your Lady Dior lasts for generations.
