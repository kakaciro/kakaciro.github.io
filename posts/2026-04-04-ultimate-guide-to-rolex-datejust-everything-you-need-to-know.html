Rolex Datejust: Overview

The Datejust by Rolex is a benchmark in horology. In-house movements and 904L stainless steel ensure decades of reliable service.

How to Authenticate Rolex Datejust

Luxury watch authentication requires checking multiple verification points:

Movement: The Datejust features in-house caliber. Second hand should function precisely.

Case Details: Serial engravings must be laser-etched. Replicas show shallow engravings.

Documentation: Box and papers should match the reference on the watch.

Rolex Datejust Price Guide

Retail and secondary market pricing for Rolex Datejust:

Retail: USD 7350 to USD 35000, varying by dial.

Secondary: Pre-owned prices range from 60-85% of retail depending on completeness.

Watch Care for Rolex Datejust

Service every 5-10 years to ensure movement accuracy. Avoid magnetic fields and extreme temperatures to maintain timekeeping precision.

Rinse with fresh water after saltwater exposure and dry with a soft cloth. Avoid chemicals and cosmetics to preserve the case finish.
