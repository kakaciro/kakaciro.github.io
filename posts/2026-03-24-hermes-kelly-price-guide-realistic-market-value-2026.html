Hermes Kelly: Overview

The Kelly is a legendary icon from the Parisian house. Craftsmen dedicate 18-24 hours of meticulous hand-stitching using premium leather.

Hermes Kelly Price Guide

The Hermes Kelly occupies a distinct position in the luxury bag market:

Retail: USD 9500 to USD 180000, varying by hardware finish and availability.

Secondary Market: Pre-owned examples typically sell for 80-120% of retail depending on completeness. Rare special order colors can exceed retail significantly.

Price Factors: Premium canvas, palladium and custom stitching affect final price.
