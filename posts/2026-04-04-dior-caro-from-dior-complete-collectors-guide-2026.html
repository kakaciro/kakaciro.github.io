Dior Dior Caro: Overview

The Dior Caro from Maison Dior showcases Gallic elegance. Meticulous embroidery and hand-finished details define its aesthetic.

Dior Dior Caro Price Guide

The Dior Dior Caro occupies a distinct position in the luxury bag market:

Retail: USD 2000 to USD 15000, varying by leather type and region.

Secondary Market: Pre-owned examples typically sell for 80-120% of retail depending on supply. Rare limited editions can exceed retail significantly.

Price Factors: Togo leather, rose gold and special color affect final price.

Care and Maintenance for Dior Dior Caro

Store your Dior Caro upright in the dust bag when not in use. Avoid exposure to direct sunlight to preserve the leather suppleness.

Clean with a soft dry cloth monthly. Condition leather every 3-6 months using only approved products to maintain the natural patina.

For hardware oxidation consult a professional. Annual professional cleaning will ensure your Dior Caro lasts for generations.
