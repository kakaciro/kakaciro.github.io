Bulgari Fiorever: Overview

The Fiorever from Bulgari showcases bold Italian design. Vibrant gemstones and sculptural forms make this an instantly recognizable icon.

Jewelry Care for Bulgari Fiorever

Store in the original box when not wearing. Apply perfume and lotions first to preserve gold luster and stone brilliance.

Clean monthly using a soft brush and mild soap and water. Avoid ultrasonic cleaners as they may damage delicate stones.

Have prongs and settings checked annually to prevent stone loss. Proper storage will keep your Fiorever sparkling for decades.
