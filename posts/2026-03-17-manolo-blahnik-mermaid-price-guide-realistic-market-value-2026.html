Manolo Blahnik Mermaid: Overview

The Mermaid from Manolo Blahnik represents the pinnacle of shoemaking art. Hand-crafted in Italy with premium leathers, it delivers timeless style.

Manolo Blahnik Mermaid Price Guide

The Manolo Blahnik Mermaid occupies a distinct position in the luxury bag market:

Retail: USD 2000 to USD 15000, varying by hardware finish and availability.

Secondary Market: Pre-owned examples typically sell for 70-95% of retail depending on supply. Rare special order colors can exceed retail significantly.

Price Factors: Togo leather, palladium and rare hardware affect final price.
