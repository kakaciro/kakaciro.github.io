How to Authenticate Patek Philippe Calatrava

The Patek Philippe Calatrava is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: 18K gold stamp must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic Calatrava uses mother of pearl with precise setting. Check for gaps in the prong setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests base metal.
