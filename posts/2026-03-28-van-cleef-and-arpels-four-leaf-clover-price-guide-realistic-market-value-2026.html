How to Authenticate Van Cleef and Arpels Four Leaf Clover

The Van Cleef and Arpels Four Leaf Clover is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: VCA hallmark must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic Four Leaf Clover uses quality stones with precise setting. Check for gaps in the claw setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests gold plating.
