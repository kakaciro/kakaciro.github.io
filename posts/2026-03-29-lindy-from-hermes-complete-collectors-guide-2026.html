Hermes Lindy: Overview

The Lindy is a legendary icon from the Parisian house. Craftsmen dedicate 18-24 hours of meticulous hand-stitching using premium leather.

Hermes Lindy Price Guide

The Hermes Lindy occupies a distinct position in the luxury bag market:

Retail: USD 2000 to USD 15000, varying by size and availability.

Secondary Market: Pre-owned examples typically sell for 100-140% of retail depending on condition. Rare special order colors can exceed retail significantly.

Price Factors: Exotic skin, gold hardware and custom stitching affect final price.
