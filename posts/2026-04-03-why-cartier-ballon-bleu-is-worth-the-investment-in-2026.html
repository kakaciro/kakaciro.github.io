Cartier Ballon Bleu: Overview

How to Authenticate Cartier Ballon Bleu

The Cartier Ballon Bleu is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: platinum 950 mark must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic Ballon Bleu uses quality stones with precise setting. Check for gaps in the claw setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests gold plating.
