How to Authenticate Van Cleef and Arpels Romeo Juliet

The Van Cleef and Arpels Romeo Juliet is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Stones: Authentic Romeo Juliet uses mother of pearl with precise setting. Check for gaps in the claw setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests base metal.
