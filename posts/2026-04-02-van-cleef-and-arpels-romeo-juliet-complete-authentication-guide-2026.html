Van Cleef and Arpels Romeo Juliet: Overview

The Romeo Juliet by Van Cleef and Arpels captures romantic elegance. Alhambra motifs are hand-set by master artisans using 18K gold.

How to Authenticate Van Cleef and Arpels Romeo Juliet

The Van Cleef and Arpels Romeo Juliet is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: VCA hallmark must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic Romeo Juliet uses mother of pearl with precise setting. Check for gaps in the bezel setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests gold plating.

Jewelry Care for Van Cleef and Arpels Romeo Juliet

Store in the original box when not wearing. Apply perfume and lotions first to preserve gold luster and stone brilliance.

Clean monthly using a soft brush and mild soap and water. Avoid ultrasonic cleaners as they may damage delicate stones.

Have prongs and settings checked annually to prevent stone loss. Proper storage will keep your Romeo Juliet sparkling for decades.
