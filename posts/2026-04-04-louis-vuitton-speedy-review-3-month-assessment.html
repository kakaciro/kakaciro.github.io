Louis Vuitton Speedy: Overview

The Speedy is a versatile house icon from Louis Vuitton. Monogram canvas construction ensures durability for daily use.

How to Authenticate Louis Vuitton Speedy

Authenticating a Louis Vuitton Speedy requires examining key details that distinguish originals from replicas:

Key Points: check the kimper, check the handles, check the trim, check the date code.

Hardware on an authentic Speedy shows precise engravings with clean edges. Counterfeits often display inconsistent spacing. Examine the leather patch under good lighting.

The edge finishing is consistent throughout. Authentic pieces use full-grain leather that develops natural character over time.

Louis Vuitton Speedy Price Guide

The Louis Vuitton Speedy occupies a distinct position in the luxury bag market:

Retail: USD 1200 to USD 2200, varying by leather type and availability.

Secondary Market: Pre-owned examples typically sell for 100-140% of retail depending on condition. Rare special order colors can exceed retail significantly.

Price Factors: Exotic skin, rose gold and special color affect final price.
