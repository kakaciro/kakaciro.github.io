Hermes Picotin Lock: Overview

The Picotin Lock is a legendary icon from the Parisian house. Craftsmen dedicate 18-24 hours of meticulous hand-stitching using premium leather.

How to Authenticate Hermes Picotin Lock

Authenticating a Hermes Picotin Lock requires examining key details that distinguish originals from replicas:

Key Points: check the craftsmanship, check the material, check the hardware, check the stitching.

Hardware on an authentic Picotin Lock shows precise engravings with clean edges. Counterfeits often display inconsistent spacing. Examine the serial placement under good lighting.

The stitching tension is consistent throughout. Authentic pieces use premium canvas that develops natural character over time.

Hermes Picotin Lock Price Guide

The Hermes Picotin Lock occupies a distinct position in the luxury bag market:

Retail: USD 2000 to USD 15000, varying by hardware finish and special order.

Secondary Market: Pre-owned examples typically sell for 80-120% of retail depending on condition. Rare special order colors can exceed retail significantly.

Price Factors: Caviar skin, palladium and rare hardware affect final price.

Care and Maintenance for Hermes Picotin Lock

Store your Picotin Lock upright in the dust bag when not in use. Avoid exposure to direct sunlight to preserve the leather suppleness.

Clean with a soft dry cloth monthly. Condition leather every 3-6 months using only approved products to maintain the natural patina.

For hardware oxidation consult a professional. Annual professional cleaning will ensure your Picotin Lock lasts for generations.
