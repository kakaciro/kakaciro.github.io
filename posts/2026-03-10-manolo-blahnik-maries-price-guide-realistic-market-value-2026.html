Manolo Blahnik Maries: Overview

The Maries from Manolo Blahnik represents the pinnacle of shoemaking art. Hand-crafted in Italy with premium leathers, it delivers timeless style.

Manolo Blahnik Maries Price Guide

The Manolo Blahnik Maries occupies a distinct position in the luxury bag market:

Retail: USD 2000 to USD 15000, varying by hardware finish and availability.

Secondary Market: Pre-owned examples typically sell for 90-130% of retail depending on supply. Rare limited editions can exceed retail significantly.

Price Factors: Togo leather, palladium and rare hardware affect final price.

Care and Maintenance for Manolo Blahnik Maries

Store your Maries upright in the dust bag when not in use. Avoid exposure to direct sunlight to preserve the leather suppleness.

Clean with a soft dry cloth monthly. Condition leather every 3-6 months using only approved products to maintain the natural patina.

For hardware oxidation consult a professional. Annual professional cleaning will ensure your Maries lasts for generations.
