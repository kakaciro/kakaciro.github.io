Dior Saddle Care and Maintenance Guide

2026-04-01

Dior Saddle: Overview

The Saddle from Maison Dior showcases Gallic elegance. Meticulous embroidery and hand-finished details define its aesthetic.

How to Authenticate Dior Saddle

Authenticating a Dior Saddle requires examining key details that distinguish originals from replicas:

Key Points: check the craftsmanship, check the material, check the hardware, check the stitching.

Hardware on an authentic Saddle shows precise engravings with clean edges. Counterfeits often display inconsistent spacing. Examine the interior tag under good lighting.

The stitching tension is consistent throughout. Authentic pieces use full-grain leather that develops natural character over time.

Care and Maintenance for Dior Saddle

Store your Saddle upright in the dust bag when not in use. Avoid exposure to direct sunlight to preserve the leather suppleness.

Clean with a soft dry cloth monthly. Condition leather every 3-6 months using only approved products to maintain the natural patina.

For hardware oxidation consult a professional. Annual professional cleaning will ensure your Saddle lasts for generations.
