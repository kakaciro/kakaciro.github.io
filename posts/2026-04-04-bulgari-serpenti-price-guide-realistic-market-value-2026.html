Bulgari Serpenti: Overview

The Serpenti from Bulgari showcases bold Italian design. Vibrant gemstones and sculptural forms make this an instantly recognizable icon.

How to Authenticate Bulgari Serpenti

The Bulgari Serpenti is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: platinum 950 mark must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic Serpenti uses mother of pearl with precise setting. Check for gaps in the claw setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests base metal.
