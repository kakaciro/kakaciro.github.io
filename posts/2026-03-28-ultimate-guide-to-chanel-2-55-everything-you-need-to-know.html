Chanel 2.55: Overview

The 2.55 represents Coco's enduring legacy. Featuring diamond-quilted leather and gilded hardware, it embodies the pinnacle of French luxury.

Chanel 2.55 Price Guide

The Chanel 2.55 occupies a distinct position in the luxury bag market:

Retail: USD 8200 to USD 16500, varying by leather type and region.

Secondary Market: Pre-owned examples typically sell for 80-120% of retail depending on color availability. Rare discontinued sizes can exceed retail significantly.

Price Factors: Caviar skin, gold hardware and special color affect final price.
