How to Authenticate Louis Vuitton Alma

Authenticating a Louis Vuitton Alma requires examining key details that distinguish originals from replicas:

Key Points: check the craftsmanship, check the material, check the hardware, check the stitching.

Hardware on an authentic Alma shows precise engravings with clean edges. Counterfeits often display inconsistent spacing. Examine the blind stamp under good lighting.

The handle flexibility is consistent throughout. Authentic pieces use full-grain leather that develops natural character over time.
