Stitching True Chanel bags feature precisely counted stitches, typically 10-12 stitches per inch on leather portions. ### 2. Hardware Quality Authentic Chanel hardware has substantial weight and features precise engravings. The back plate of the turn-lock should read CHANEL + Paris. ### 3. Interior Logo The interior logo plate should be perfectly centered with clean, sharp engraving. Replicas often show faded or misaligned text. ### 4. Leather Quality Chanel uses premium lambskin that feels supple yet substantial. The grain should be consistent and the leather should have a distinct, high-end feel. ## Price Comparison (2026) Both bags now retail for $10,200+ and have waiting lists at boutique locations. womanlvbag.com
