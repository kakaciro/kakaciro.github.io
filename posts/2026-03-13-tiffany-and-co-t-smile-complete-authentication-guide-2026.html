Tiffany and Co T Smile: Overview

The T Smile from Tiffany and Co represents 184 years of heritage. 18K gold and precise stone selection create truly iconic pieces.

How to Authenticate Tiffany and Co T Smile

The Tiffany and Co T Smile is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: serial engraving must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic T Smile uses VVS diamonds with precise setting. Check for gaps in the bezel setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests base metal.
