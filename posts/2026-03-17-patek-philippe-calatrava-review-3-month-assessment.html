Patek Philippe Calatrava: Overview

The Calatrava by Patek Philippe represents the pinnacle of watchmaking. In-house calibers create a timepiece meant to outlast generations.

Patek Philippe Calatrava Price Guide

Jewelry pricing depends on stone selection:

Retail: USD 2000 to USD 15000, varying by bracelet size and collection.

Secondary Market: estate pieces typically retains 40-60% of retail. out-of-production designs command premiums.

Jewelry Care for Patek Philippe Calatrava

Store in the original box when not wearing. Apply perfume and lotions first to preserve gold luster and stone brilliance.

Clean monthly using a soft brush and mild soap and water. Avoid ultrasonic cleaners as they may damage delicate stones.

Have prongs and settings checked annually to prevent stone loss. Proper storage will keep your Calatrava sparkling for decades.
