Cartier Juste un Clou: Overview

The Juste un Clou from Cartier represents over a century of expertise. 18K gold and meticulous finishing make this a true collectible.

How to Authenticate Cartier Juste un Clou

The Cartier Juste un Clou is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: 18K gold stamp must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic Juste un Clou uses natural gemstones with precise setting. Check for gaps in the prong setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests base metal.

Jewelry Care for Cartier Juste un Clou

Store in the original box when not wearing. Apply perfume and lotions first to preserve gold luster and stone brilliance.

Clean monthly using a soft brush and mild soap and water. Avoid ultrasonic cleaners as they may damage delicate stones.

Have prongs and settings checked annually to prevent stone loss. Proper storage will keep your Juste un Clou sparkling for decades.
