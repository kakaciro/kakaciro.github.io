Bulgari Fiorever: Overview

The Fiorever from Bulgari showcases bold Italian design. Vibrant gemstones and sculptural forms make this an instantly recognizable icon.

How to Authenticate Bulgari Fiorever

The Bulgari Fiorever is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: VCA hallmark must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic Fiorever uses VVS diamonds with precise setting. Check for gaps in the claw setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests hollow construction.
