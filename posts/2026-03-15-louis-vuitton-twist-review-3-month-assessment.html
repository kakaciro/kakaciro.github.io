Louis Vuitton Twist: Overview

The Twist is a versatile house icon from Louis Vuitton. Monogram canvas construction ensures durability for daily use.

Louis Vuitton Twist Price Guide

The Louis Vuitton Twist occupies a distinct position in the luxury bag market:

Retail: USD 2000 to USD 15000, varying by hardware finish and special order.

Secondary Market: Pre-owned examples typically sell for 100-140% of retail depending on color availability. Rare discontinued sizes can exceed retail significantly.

Price Factors: Togo leather, rose gold and custom stitching affect final price.
