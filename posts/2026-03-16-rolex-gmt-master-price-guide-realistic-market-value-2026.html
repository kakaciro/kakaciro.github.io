Rolex GMT-Master: Overview

The GMT-Master by Rolex is a benchmark in horology. In-house movements and 904L stainless steel ensure decades of reliable service.

How to Authenticate Rolex GMT-Master

Luxury watch authentication requires checking multiple verification points:

Movement: The GMT-Master features precision movement. bezel alignment should function precisely.

Case Details: Serial engravings must be perfectly aligned. Replicas show inconsistent spacing.

Documentation: Box and papers should match the movement on the watch.

Watch Care for Rolex GMT-Master

Service every 5-10 years to ensure movement accuracy. Avoid magnetic fields and extreme temperatures to maintain timekeeping precision.

Rinse with fresh water after saltwater exposure and dry with a soft cloth. Avoid chemicals and cosmetics to preserve the case finish.
