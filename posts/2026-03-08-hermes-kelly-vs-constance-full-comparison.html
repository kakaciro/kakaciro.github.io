Hermes Kelly Price Guide

Care and Maintenance for Hermes Kelly

Store your Kelly upright in the dust bag when not in use. Avoid exposure to direct sunlight to preserve the leather suppleness.

Clean with a soft dry cloth monthly. Condition leather every 3-6 months using only approved products to maintain the natural patina.

For hardware oxidation consult a professional. Annual professional cleaning will ensure your Kelly lasts for generations.
