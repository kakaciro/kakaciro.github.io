Dior Addict: Overview

The Addict from Maison Dior showcases Gallic elegance. Meticulous embroidery and hand-finished details define its aesthetic.

Dior Addict Price Guide

The Dior Addict occupies a distinct position in the luxury bag market:

Retail: USD 2000 to USD 15000, varying by size and availability.

Secondary Market: Pre-owned examples typically sell for 70-95% of retail depending on condition. Rare discontinued sizes can exceed retail significantly.

Price Factors: Exotic skin, palladium and special color affect final price.
