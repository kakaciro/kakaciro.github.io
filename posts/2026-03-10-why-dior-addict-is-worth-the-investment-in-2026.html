Dior Addict: Overview

The Addict from Maison Dior showcases Gallic elegance. Meticulous embroidery and hand-finished details define its aesthetic.

How to Authenticate Dior Addict

Authenticating a Dior Addict requires examining key details that distinguish originals from replicas:

Key Points: check the craftsmanship, check the material, check the hardware, check the stitching.

Hardware on an authentic Addict shows precise engravings with clean edges. Counterfeits often display inconsistent spacing. Examine the blind stamp under good lighting.

The stitching tension is consistent throughout. Authentic pieces use hand-stitched linen that develops natural character over time.
