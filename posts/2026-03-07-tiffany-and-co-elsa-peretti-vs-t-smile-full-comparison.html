How to Authenticate Tiffany and Co Elsa Peretti

The Tiffany and Co Elsa Peretti is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: VCA hallmark must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic Elsa Peretti uses natural gemstones with precise setting. Check for gaps in the claw setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests hollow construction.

Jewelry Care for Tiffany and Co Elsa Peretti

Clean monthly using a soft brush and mild soap and water. Avoid ultrasonic cleaners as they may damage delicate stones.

Have prongs and settings checked annually to prevent stone loss. Proper storage will keep your Elsa Peretti sparkling for decades.
