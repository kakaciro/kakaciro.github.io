Bulgari Serpenti: Overview

The Serpenti from Bulgari showcases bold Italian design. Vibrant gemstones and sculptural forms make this an instantly recognizable icon.

How to Authenticate Bulgari Serpenti

The Bulgari Serpenti is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: serial engraving must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic Serpenti uses VVS diamonds with precise setting. Check for gaps in the claw setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests base metal.

Bulgari Serpenti Price Guide

Jewelry pricing depends on piece type:

Retail: USD 4500 to USD 35000, varying by stone carat and collection.

Secondary Market: Gold jewelry typically retains 60-80% of retail. Vintage pieces command premiums.

Jewelry Care for Bulgari Serpenti

Store in the original box when not wearing. Apply perfume and lotions first to preserve gold luster and stone brilliance.

Clean monthly using a soft brush and mild soap and water. Avoid ultrasonic cleaners as they may damage delicate stones.

Have prongs and settings checked annually to prevent stone loss. Proper storage will keep your Serpenti sparkling for decades.
