How to Authenticate Patek Philippe Grand Complications

The Patek Philippe Grand Complications is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: platinum 950 mark must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic Grand Complications uses VVS diamonds with precise setting. Check for gaps in the claw setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests gold plating.
