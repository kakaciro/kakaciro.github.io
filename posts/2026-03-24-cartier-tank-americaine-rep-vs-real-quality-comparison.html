How to Authenticate Cartier Tank Americaine

The Cartier Tank Americaine is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: VCA hallmark must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic Tank Americaine uses quality stones with precise setting. Check for gaps in the bezel setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests base metal.

Jewelry Care for Cartier Tank Americaine

Store in the original box when not wearing. Apply perfume and lotions first to preserve gold luster and stone brilliance.

Have prongs and settings checked annually to prevent stone loss. Proper storage will keep your Tank Americaine sparkling for decades.
