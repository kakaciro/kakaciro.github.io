Hermes Picotin Lock: Overview

The Picotin Lock is a legendary icon from the Parisian house. Craftsmen dedicate 18-24 hours of meticulous hand-stitching using premium leather.

How to Authenticate Hermes Picotin Lock

Authenticating a Hermes Picotin Lock requires examining key details that distinguish originals from replicas:

Key Points: check the craftsmanship, check the material, check the hardware, check the stitching.

Hardware on an authentic Picotin Lock shows precise engravings with clean edges. Counterfeits often display inconsistent spacing. Examine the interior tag under good lighting.

The handle flexibility is consistent throughout. Authentic pieces use hand-stitched linen that develops natural character over time.
