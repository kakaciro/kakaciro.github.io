Louis Vuitton Onthego: Overview

The Onthego is a versatile house icon from Louis Vuitton. Monogram canvas construction ensures durability for daily use.

Care and Maintenance for Louis Vuitton Onthego

Store your Onthego upright in the dust bag when not in use. Avoid exposure to direct sunlight to preserve the leather suppleness.

Clean with a soft dry cloth monthly. Condition leather every 3-6 months using only approved products to maintain the natural patina.

For hardware oxidation consult a professional. Annual professional cleaning will ensure your Onthego lasts for generations.
