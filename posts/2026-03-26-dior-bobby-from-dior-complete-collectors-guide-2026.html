Dior Dior Bobby: Overview

The Dior Bobby from Maison Dior showcases Gallic elegance. Meticulous embroidery and hand-finished details define its aesthetic.

Care and Maintenance for Dior Dior Bobby

Store your Dior Bobby upright in the dust bag when not in use. Avoid exposure to direct sunlight to preserve the leather suppleness.

Clean with a soft dry cloth monthly. Condition leather every 3-6 months using only approved products to maintain the natural patina.

For hardware oxidation consult a professional. Annual professional cleaning will ensure your Dior Bobby lasts for generations.
