Dior Addict: Overview

The Addict from Maison Dior showcases Gallic elegance. Meticulous embroidery and hand-finished details define its aesthetic.

How to Authenticate Dior Addict

Authenticating a Dior Addict requires examining key details that distinguish originals from replicas:

Key Points: check the craftsmanship, check the material, check the hardware, check the stitching.

Hardware on an authentic Addict shows precise engravings with clean edges. Counterfeits often display inconsistent spacing. Examine the date code under good lighting.

The stitching tension is consistent throughout. Authentic pieces use hand-stitched linen that develops natural character over time.

Dior Addict Price Guide

The Dior Addict occupies a distinct position in the luxury bag market:

Retail: USD 2000 to USD 15000, varying by hardware finish and region.

Secondary Market: Pre-owned examples typically sell for 90-130% of retail depending on completeness. Rare discontinued sizes can exceed retail significantly.

Price Factors: Togo leather, gold hardware and special color affect final price.

Care and Maintenance for Dior Addict

Store your Addict upright in the dust bag when not in use. Avoid exposure to direct sunlight to preserve the leather suppleness.

Clean with a soft dry cloth monthly. Condition leather every 3-6 months using only approved products to maintain the natural patina.

For hardware oxidation consult a professional. Annual professional cleaning will ensure your Addict lasts for generations.
