Hermes Picotin Lock: Overview

The Picotin Lock is a legendary icon from the Parisian house. Craftsmen dedicate 18-24 hours of meticulous hand-stitching using premium leather.

How to Authenticate Hermes Picotin Lock

Authenticating a Hermes Picotin Lock requires examining key details that distinguish originals from replicas:

Key Points: check the craftsmanship, check the material, check the hardware, check the stitching.

Hardware on an authentic Picotin Lock shows precise engravings with clean edges. Counterfeits often display inconsistent spacing. Examine the blind stamp under good lighting.

The handle flexibility is consistent throughout. Authentic pieces use premium canvas that develops natural character over time.

Hermes Picotin Lock Price Guide

The Hermes Picotin Lock occupies a distinct position in the luxury bag market:

Retail: USD 2000 to USD 15000, varying by leather type and region.

Secondary Market: Pre-owned examples typically sell for 80-120% of retail depending on color availability. Rare special order colors can exceed retail significantly.

Price Factors: Exotic skin, gold hardware and special color affect final price.
