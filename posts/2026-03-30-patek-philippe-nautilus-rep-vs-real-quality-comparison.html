How to Authenticate Patek Philippe Nautilus

The Patek Philippe Nautilus is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: 18K gold stamp must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic Nautilus uses natural gemstones with precise setting. Check for gaps in the claw setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests hollow construction.
