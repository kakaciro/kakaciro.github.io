Manolo Blahnik Mermaid: Overview

The Mermaid from Manolo Blahnik represents the pinnacle of shoemaking art. Hand-crafted in Italy with premium leathers, it delivers timeless style.

How to Authenticate Manolo Blahnik Mermaid

Authenticating a Manolo Blahnik Mermaid requires examining key details that distinguish originals from replicas:

Key Points: check the craftsmanship, check the material, check the hardware, check the stitching.

Hardware on an authentic Mermaid shows precise engravings with clean edges. Counterfeits often display inconsistent spacing. Examine the date code under good lighting.

The handle flexibility is consistent throughout. Authentic pieces use full-grain leather that develops natural character over time.

Manolo Blahnik Mermaid Price Guide

The Manolo Blahnik Mermaid occupies a distinct position in the luxury bag market:

Retail: USD 2000 to USD 15000, varying by size and availability.

Secondary Market: Pre-owned examples typically sell for 100-140% of retail depending on color availability. Rare discontinued sizes can exceed retail significantly.

Price Factors: Exotic skin, rose gold and rare hardware affect final price.
