Cartier Panthere: Overview

The Panthere from Cartier represents over a century of expertise. 18K gold and meticulous finishing make this a true collectible.

How to Authenticate Cartier Panthere

The Cartier Panthere is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: VCA hallmark must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic Panthere uses VVS diamonds with precise setting. Check for gaps in the prong setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests gold plating.

Cartier Panthere Price Guide

Jewelry pricing depends on metal type:

Retail: USD 8900 to USD 24000, varying by design complexity and availability.

Secondary Market: fine jewelry typically retains 50-70% of retail. limited editions command premiums.

Jewelry Care for Cartier Panthere

Store in the original box when not wearing. Apply perfume and lotions first to preserve gold luster and stone brilliance.

Clean monthly using a soft brush and mild soap and water. Avoid ultrasonic cleaners as they may damage delicate stones.

Have prongs and settings checked annually to prevent stone loss. Proper storage will keep your Panthere sparkling for decades.
