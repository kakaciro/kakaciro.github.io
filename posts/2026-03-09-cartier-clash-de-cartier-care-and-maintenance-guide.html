Cartier Clash de Cartier: Overview

The Clash de Cartier from Cartier represents over a century of expertise. 18K gold and meticulous finishing make this a true collectible.

How to Authenticate Cartier Clash de Cartier

The Cartier Clash de Cartier is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: VCA hallmark must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic Clash de Cartier uses mother of pearl with precise setting. Check for gaps in the claw setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests gold plating.

Jewelry Care for Cartier Clash de Cartier

Store in the original box when not wearing. Apply perfume and lotions first to preserve gold luster and stone brilliance.

Clean monthly using a soft brush and mild soap and water. Avoid ultrasonic cleaners as they may damage delicate stones.

Have prongs and settings checked annually to prevent stone loss. Proper storage will keep your Clash de Cartier sparkling for decades.
