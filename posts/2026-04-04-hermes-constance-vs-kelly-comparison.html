Hermès Constance vs Kelly: Full Comparison

Published: April 04, 2026 | Category: Comparison

Two iconic H-bags, very different personalities. Here's the breakdown.

Size Comparison

Constance

  • Dimensions: 14" x 12" x 5"
  • Single compartment with flat interior
  • More suitable for daily use

Kelly

  • Dimensions: 14" x 12" x 7"
  • Structured with interior pockets
  • More formal presence

The H-Clasp

Both feature the iconic turn-lock, but:

  • Kelly's clasp is larger and more ornate
  • Constance has a smaller, simpler version
  • Both require precise alignment to close

Status Factor

Kelly: Longer waitlist, higher prestige

  • Takes 1-3 years to get from boutique
  • Higher resell value

Constance: Slightly more accessible

  • Waitlist typically 6-18 months
  • Still carries massive prestige
