Dior Bobby: Overview

The Bobby from Maison Dior showcases Gallic elegance. Meticulous embroidery and hand-finished details define its aesthetic.

How to Authenticate Dior Bobby

Authenticating a Dior Bobby requires examining key details that distinguish originals from replicas:

Key Points: check the craftsmanship, check the material, check the hardware, check the stitching.

Hardware on an authentic Bobby shows precise engravings with clean edges. Counterfeits often display inconsistent spacing. Examine the interior tag under good lighting.

The stitching tension is consistent throughout. Authentic pieces use premium canvas that develops natural character over time.

Dior Bobby Price Guide

The Dior Bobby occupies a distinct position in the luxury bag market:

Retail: USD 2000 to USD 15000, varying by leather type and availability.

Secondary Market: Pre-owned examples typically sell for 80-120% of retail depending on color availability. Rare discontinued sizes can exceed retail significantly.

Price Factors: Exotic skin, gold hardware and custom stitching affect final price.
