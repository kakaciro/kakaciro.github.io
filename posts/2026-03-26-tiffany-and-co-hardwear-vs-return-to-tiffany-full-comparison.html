How to Authenticate Tiffany and Co HardWear

The Tiffany and Co HardWear is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: serial engraving must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic HardWear uses VVS diamonds with precise setting. Check for gaps in the bezel setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests hollow construction.

Clean monthly using a soft brush and mild soap and water. Avoid ultrasonic cleaners as they may damage delicate stones.

Have prongs and settings checked annually to prevent stone loss. Proper storage will keep your HardWear sparkling for decades.
