How to Authenticate Dior Dior Caro

Authenticating a Dior Dior Caro requires examining key details that distinguish originals from replicas:

Key Points: check the craftsmanship, check the material, check the hardware, check the stitching.

Hardware on an authentic Dior Caro shows precise engravings with clean edges. Counterfeits often display inconsistent spacing. Examine the leather patch under good lighting.

The edge finishing is consistent throughout. Authentic pieces use premium canvas that develops natural character over time.
