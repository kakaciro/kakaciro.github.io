Bulgari Lvcea: Overview

The Lvcea from Bulgari showcases bold Italian design. Vibrant gemstones and sculptural forms make this an instantly recognizable icon.

How to Authenticate Bulgari Lvcea

The Bulgari Lvcea is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: VCA hallmark must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic Lvcea uses mother of pearl with precise setting. Check for gaps in the prong setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests base metal.

Jewelry Care for Bulgari Lvcea

Store in the original box when not wearing. Apply perfume and lotions first to preserve gold luster and stone brilliance.

Clean monthly using a soft brush and mild soap and water. Avoid ultrasonic cleaners as they may damage delicate stones.

Have prongs and settings checked annually to prevent stone loss. Proper storage will keep your Lvcea sparkling for decades.
