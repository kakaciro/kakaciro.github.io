Dior Dior Caro: Overview

The Dior Caro from Maison Dior showcases Gallic elegance. Meticulous embroidery and hand-finished details define its aesthetic.

Dior Dior Caro Price Guide

The Dior Dior Caro occupies a distinct position in the luxury bag market:

Retail: USD 2000 to USD 15000, varying by seasonal collection and availability.

Secondary Market: Pre-owned examples typically sell for 100-150% of retail depending on color availability. Rare discontinued sizes can exceed retail significantly.

Price Factors: Premium canvas, rose gold and rare hardware affect final price.
