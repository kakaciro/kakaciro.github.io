Patek Philippe Aquanaut: Overview

The Aquanaut by Patek Philippe represents the pinnacle of watchmaking. In-house calibers create a timepiece meant to outlast generations.

How to Authenticate Patek Philippe Aquanaut

The Patek Philippe Aquanaut is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: platinum 950 mark must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic Aquanaut uses mother of pearl with precise setting. Check for gaps in the prong setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests hollow construction.
