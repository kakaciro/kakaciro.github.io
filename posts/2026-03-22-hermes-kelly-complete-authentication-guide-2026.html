Hermes Kelly: Overview

The Kelly is a legendary icon from the Parisian house. Craftsmen dedicate 18-24 hours of meticulous hand-stitching using premium leather.

How to Authenticate Hermes Kelly

Authenticating a Hermes Kelly requires examining key details that distinguish originals from replicas:

Key Points: check the R socket, check the clochette, check the handle symmetry, check the hardware engravings.

Hardware on an authentic Kelly shows precise engravings with clean edges. Counterfeits often display inconsistent spacing. Examine the interior tag under good lighting.

The handle flexibility is consistent throughout. Authentic pieces use hand-stitched linen that develops natural character over time.
