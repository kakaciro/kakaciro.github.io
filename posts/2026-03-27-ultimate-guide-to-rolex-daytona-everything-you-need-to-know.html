Rolex Daytona: Overview

The Daytona by Rolex is a benchmark in horology. In-house movements and 904L stainless steel ensure decades of reliable service.

How to Authenticate Rolex Daytona

Luxury watch authentication requires checking multiple verification points:

Movement: The Daytona features COSC certification. bezel alignment should function precisely.

Case Details: model number must be deeply engraved. Replicas show shallow engravings.

Documentation: Box and papers should match the serial number on the watch.

Rolex Daytona Price Guide

Retail and secondary market pricing for Rolex Daytona:

Retail: USD 14500 to USD 180000, varying by reference.

Secondary: Pre-owned prices range from 60-85% of retail depending on completeness.
