Cartier Tank Louis Care and Maintenance Guide

2026-03-26

Cartier Tank Louis: Overview

The Tank Louis from Cartier represents over a century of expertise. 18K gold and meticulous finishing make this a true collectible.

Jewelry Care for Cartier Tank Louis

Store in the original box when not wearing. Apply perfume and lotions first to preserve gold luster and stone brilliance.

Clean monthly using a soft brush and mild soap and water. Avoid ultrasonic cleaners as they may damage delicate stones.

Have prongs and settings checked annually to prevent stone loss. Proper storage will keep your Tank Louis sparkling for decades.
