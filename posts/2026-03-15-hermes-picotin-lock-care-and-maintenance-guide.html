Hermes Picotin Lock: Overview

The Picotin Lock is a legendary icon from the Parisian house. Craftsmen dedicate 18-24 hours of meticulous hand-stitching using premium leather.

Hermes Picotin Lock Price Guide

The Hermes Picotin Lock occupies a distinct position in the luxury bag market:

Retail: USD 2000 to USD 15000, varying by hardware finish and special order.

Secondary Market: Pre-owned examples typically sell for 80-120% of retail depending on supply. Rare discontinued sizes can exceed retail significantly.

Price Factors: Togo leather, palladium and rare hardware affect final price.
