How to Authenticate Van Cleef and Arpels Vintage Alhambra

The Van Cleef and Arpels Vintage Alhambra is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: platinum 950 mark must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic Vintage Alhambra uses quality stones with precise setting. Check for gaps in the prong setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests base metal.
