Cartier Santos: Overview

The Santos from Cartier represents over a century of expertise. 18K gold and meticulous finishing make this a true collectible.

How to Authenticate Cartier Santos

The Cartier Santos is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: platinum 950 mark must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic Santos uses quality stones with precise setting. Check for gaps in the claw setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests base metal.

Cartier Santos Price Guide

Jewelry pricing depends on collection year:

Retail: USD 2000 to USD 15000, varying by design complexity and collection.

Secondary Market: fine jewelry typically retains 40-60% of retail. limited editions command premiums.

Jewelry Care for Cartier Santos

Store in the original box when not wearing. Apply perfume and lotions first to preserve gold luster and stone brilliance.

Clean monthly using a soft brush and mild soap and water. Avoid ultrasonic cleaners as they may damage delicate stones.

Have prongs and settings checked annually to prevent stone loss. Proper storage will keep your Santos sparkling for decades.
