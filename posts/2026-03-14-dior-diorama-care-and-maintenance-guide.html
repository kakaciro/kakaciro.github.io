Dior Diorama: Overview

The Diorama from Maison Dior showcases Gallic elegance. Meticulous embroidery and hand-finished details define its aesthetic.

Dior Diorama Price Guide

The Dior Diorama occupies a distinct position in the luxury bag market:

Retail: USD 2000 to USD 15000, varying by hardware finish and special order.

Secondary Market: Pre-owned examples typically sell for 70-95% of retail depending on condition. Rare special order colors can exceed retail significantly.

Price Factors: Exotic skin, gold hardware and special color affect final price.

Care and Maintenance for Dior Diorama

Store your Diorama upright in the dust bag when not in use. Avoid exposure to direct sunlight to preserve the leather suppleness.

Clean with a soft dry cloth monthly. Condition leather every 3-6 months using only approved products to maintain the natural patina.

For hardware oxidation consult a professional. Annual professional cleaning will ensure your Diorama lasts for generations.
