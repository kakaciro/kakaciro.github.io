How to Authenticate Jimmy Choo Bettina

Authenticating a Jimmy Choo Bettina requires examining key details that distinguish originals from replicas:

Key Points: check the craftsmanship, check the material, check the hardware, check the stitching.

Hardware on an authentic Bettina shows precise engravings with clean edges. Counterfeits often display inconsistent spacing. Examine the date code under good lighting.

The edge finishing is consistent throughout. Authentic pieces use full-grain leather that develops natural character over time.
