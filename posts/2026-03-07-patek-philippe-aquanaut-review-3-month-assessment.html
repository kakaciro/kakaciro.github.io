Patek Philippe Aquanaut: Overview

The Aquanaut by Patek Philippe represents the pinnacle of watchmaking. In-house calibers create a timepiece meant to outlast generations.

How to Authenticate Patek Philippe Aquanaut

The Patek Philippe Aquanaut is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: VCA hallmark must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic Aquanaut uses natural gemstones with precise setting. Check for gaps in the claw setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests base metal.

Jewelry Care for Patek Philippe Aquanaut

Store in the original box when not wearing. Apply perfume and lotions first to preserve gold luster and stone brilliance.

Clean monthly using a soft brush and mild soap and water. Avoid ultrasonic cleaners as they may damage delicate stones.

Have prongs and settings checked annually to prevent stone loss. Proper storage will keep your Aquanaut sparkling for decades.
