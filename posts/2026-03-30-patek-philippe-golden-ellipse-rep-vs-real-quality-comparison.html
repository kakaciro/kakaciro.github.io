How to Authenticate Patek Philippe Golden Ellipse

The Patek Philippe Golden Ellipse is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: serial engraving must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic Golden Ellipse uses natural gemstones with precise setting. Check for gaps in the bezel setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests hollow construction.

Jewelry Care for Patek Philippe Golden Ellipse

Store in the original box when not wearing. Apply perfume and lotions first to preserve gold luster and stone brilliance.

Clean monthly using a soft brush and mild soap and water. Avoid ultrasonic cleaners as they may damage delicate stones.

Have prongs and settings checked annually to prevent stone loss. Proper storage will keep your Golden Ellipse sparkling for decades.
