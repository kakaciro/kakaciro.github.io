Chanel Cambon: Overview

The Cambon represents Coco's enduring legacy. Featuring diamond-quilted leather and gilded hardware, it embodies the pinnacle of French luxury.

Chanel Cambon Price Guide

The Chanel Cambon occupies a distinct position in the luxury bag market:

Retail: USD 2000 to USD 15000, varying by hardware finish and region.

Secondary Market: Pre-owned examples typically sell for 80-120% of retail depending on completeness. Rare discontinued sizes can exceed retail significantly.

Price Factors: Caviar skin, gold hardware and rare hardware affect final price.
