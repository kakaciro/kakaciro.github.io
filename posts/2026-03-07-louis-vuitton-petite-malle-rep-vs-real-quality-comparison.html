How to Authenticate Louis Vuitton Petite Malle

Authenticating a Louis Vuitton Petite Malle requires examining key details that distinguish originals from replicas:

Key Points: check the craftsmanship, check the material, check the hardware, check the stitching.

Hardware on an authentic Petite Malle shows precise engravings with clean edges. Counterfeits often display inconsistent spacing. Examine the leather patch under good lighting.

The edge finishing is consistent throughout. Authentic pieces use full-grain leather that develops natural character over time.
