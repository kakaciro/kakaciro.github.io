How to Authenticate Cartier Juste un Clou

The Cartier Juste un Clou is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Stones: Authentic Juste un Clou uses mother of pearl with precise setting. Check for gaps in the prong setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests gold plating.
