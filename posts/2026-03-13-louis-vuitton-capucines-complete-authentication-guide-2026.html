How to Authenticate Louis Vuitton Capucines

Authenticating a Louis Vuitton Capucines requires examining key details that distinguish originals from replicas:

Key Points: check the craftsmanship, check the material, check the hardware, check the stitching.

Hardware on an authentic Capucines shows precise engravings with clean edges. Counterfeits often display inconsistent spacing. Examine the leather patch under good lighting.

The stitching tension is consistent throughout. Authentic pieces use hand-stitched linen that develops natural character over time.

Louis Vuitton Capucines Price Guide
