Tiffany and Co Knot: Overview

The Knot from Tiffany and Co represents 184 years of heritage. 18K gold and precise stone selection create truly iconic pieces.

How to Authenticate Tiffany and Co Knot

The Tiffany and Co Knot is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: VCA hallmark must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic Knot uses natural gemstones with precise setting. Check for gaps in the bezel setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests hollow construction.
