How to Authenticate Bulgari Lvcea

The Bulgari Lvcea is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: serial engraving must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic Lvcea uses quality stones with precise setting. Check for gaps in the claw setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests hollow construction.
