Dior Cannage: Overview

The Cannage from Maison Dior showcases Gallic elegance. Meticulous embroidery and hand-finished details define its aesthetic.

Dior Cannage Price Guide

The Dior Cannage occupies a distinct position in the luxury bag market:

Retail: USD 2000 to USD 15000, varying by hardware finish and availability.

Secondary Market: Pre-owned examples typically sell for 70-95% of retail depending on color availability. Rare special order colors can exceed retail significantly.

Price Factors: Premium canvas, gold hardware and custom stitching affect final price.
