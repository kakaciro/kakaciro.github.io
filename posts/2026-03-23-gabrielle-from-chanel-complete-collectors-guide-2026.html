Chanel Gabrielle: Overview

The Gabrielle represents Coco's enduring legacy. Featuring diamond-quilted leather and gilded hardware, it embodies the pinnacle of French luxury.

Chanel Gabrielle Price Guide

The Chanel Gabrielle occupies a distinct position in the luxury bag market:

Retail: USD 2000 to USD 15000, varying by leather type and region.

Secondary Market: Pre-owned examples typically sell for 100-140% of retail depending on supply. Rare limited editions can exceed retail significantly.

Price Factors: Premium canvas, rose gold and special color affect final price.
