Hermes Constance: Overview

The Constance is a legendary icon from the Parisian house. Craftsmen dedicate 18-24 hours of meticulous hand-stitching using premium leather.

How to Authenticate Hermes Constance

Authenticating a Hermes Constance requires examining key details that distinguish originals from replicas:

Key Points: check the H clasp, check the strap attachment, check the EPC pocket, check the blind stamp.

Hardware on an authentic Constance shows precise engravings with clean edges. Counterfeits often display inconsistent spacing. Examine the blind stamp under good lighting.

The handle flexibility is consistent throughout. Authentic pieces use full-grain leather that develops natural character over time.

Hermes Constance Price Guide

The Hermes Constance occupies a distinct position in the luxury bag market:

Retail: USD 7500 to USD 45000, varying by hardware finish and special order.

Secondary Market: Pre-owned examples typically sell for 80-120% of retail depending on condition. Rare discontinued sizes can exceed retail significantly.

Price Factors: Caviar skin, rose gold and special color affect final price.

Care and Maintenance for Hermes Constance

Store your Constance upright in the dust bag when not in use. Avoid exposure to direct sunlight to preserve the leather suppleness.

Clean with a soft dry cloth monthly. Condition leather every 3-6 months using only approved products to maintain the natural patina.

For hardware oxidation consult a professional. Annual professional cleaning will ensure your Constance lasts for generations.
