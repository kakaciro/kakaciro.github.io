Hermes Evelyne: Overview

The Evelyne is a legendary icon from the Parisian house. Craftsmen dedicate 18-24 hours of meticulous hand-stitching using premium leather.

Hermes Evelyne Price Guide

The Hermes Evelyne occupies a distinct position in the luxury bag market:

Retail: USD 2000 to USD 15000, varying by size and availability.

Secondary Market: Pre-owned examples typically sell for 100-150% of retail depending on completeness. Rare discontinued sizes can exceed retail significantly.

Price Factors: Premium canvas, palladium and custom stitching affect final price.

Care and Maintenance for Hermes Evelyne

Store your Evelyne upright in the dust bag when not in use. Avoid exposure to direct sunlight to preserve the leather suppleness.

Clean with a soft dry cloth monthly. Condition leather every 3-6 months using only approved products to maintain the natural patina.

For hardware oxidation consult a professional. Annual professional cleaning will ensure your Evelyne lasts for generations.
