How to Authenticate Louis Vuitton Onthego

Authenticating a Louis Vuitton Onthego requires examining key details that distinguish originals from replicas:

Key Points: check the craftsmanship, check the material, check the hardware, check the stitching.

Hardware on an authentic Onthego shows precise engravings with clean edges. Counterfeits often display inconsistent spacing. Examine the interior tag under good lighting.

The stitching tension is consistent throughout. Authentic pieces use hand-stitched linen that develops natural character over time.
