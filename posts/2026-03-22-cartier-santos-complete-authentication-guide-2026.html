Cartier Santos: Overview

The Santos from Cartier represents over a century of expertise. 18K gold and meticulous finishing make this a true collectible.

How to Authenticate Cartier Santos

The Cartier Santos is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: platinum 950 mark must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic Santos uses natural gemstones with precise setting. Check for gaps in the bezel setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests gold plating.
