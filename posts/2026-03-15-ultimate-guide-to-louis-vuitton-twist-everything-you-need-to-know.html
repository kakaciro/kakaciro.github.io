The Louis Vuitton Twist occupies a distinct position in the luxury bag market:

Secondary Market: Pre-owned examples typically sell for 100-140% of retail depending on supply. Rare limited editions can exceed retail significantly.

Price Factors: Premium canvas, gold hardware and rare hardware affect final price.
