Manolo Blahnik Hangisi: Overview

The Hangisi from Manolo Blahnik represents the pinnacle of shoemaking art. Hand-crafted in Italy with premium leathers, it delivers timeless style.

Manolo Blahnik Hangisi Price Guide

The Manolo Blahnik Hangisi occupies a distinct position in the luxury bag market:

Retail: USD 2000 to USD 15000, varying by size and availability.

Secondary Market: Pre-owned examples typically sell for 100-150% of retail depending on color availability. Rare discontinued sizes can exceed retail significantly.

Price Factors: Premium canvas, palladium and special color affect final price.

Care and Maintenance for Manolo Blahnik Hangisi

Store your Hangisi upright in the dust bag when not in use. Avoid exposure to direct sunlight to preserve the leather suppleness.

Clean with a soft dry cloth monthly. Condition leather every 3-6 months using only approved products to maintain the natural patina.

For hardware oxidation consult a professional. Annual professional cleaning will ensure your Hangisi lasts for generations.
