Patek Philippe Twenty-4: Overview

The Twenty-4 by Patek Philippe represents the pinnacle of watchmaking. In-house calibers create a timepiece meant to outlast generations.

How to Authenticate Patek Philippe Twenty-4

The Patek Philippe Twenty-4 is frequently counterfeited. Here is how to verify authenticity:

Hallmark: VCA hallmark must be present and professionally executed. Thin plating on fakes wears quickly.

Stones: Authentic Twenty-4 uses VVS diamonds with precise setting. Check for gaps in the prong setting.

Weight: Authentic pieces have substantial feel. Unusually light weight suggests gold plating.

Patek Philippe Twenty-4 Price Guide

Jewelry pricing depends on collection year:

Retail: USD 2000 to USD 15000, varying by bracelet size and collection.

Secondary Market: Gold jewelry typically retains 40-60% of retail. Vintage pieces command premiums.

Jewelry Care for Patek Philippe Twenty-4

Store in the original box when not wearing. Apply perfume and lotions first to preserve gold luster and stone brilliance.

Clean monthly using a soft brush and mild soap and water. Avoid ultrasonic cleaners as they may damage delicate stones.

Have prongs and settings checked annually to prevent stone loss. Proper storage will keep your Twenty-4 sparkling for decades.
