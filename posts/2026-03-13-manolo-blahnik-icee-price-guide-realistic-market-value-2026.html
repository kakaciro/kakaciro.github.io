Manolo Blahnik ICEE: Overview

The ICEE from Manolo Blahnik represents the pinnacle of shoemaking art. Hand-crafted in Italy with premium leathers, it delivers timeless style.

Manolo Blahnik ICEE Price Guide

The Manolo Blahnik ICEE occupies a distinct position in the luxury bag market:

Retail: USD 2000 to USD 15000, varying by seasonal collection and special order.

Secondary Market: Pre-owned examples typically sell for 80-120% of retail depending on supply. Rare limited editions can exceed retail significantly.

Price Factors: Togo leather, rose gold and custom stitching affect final price.
