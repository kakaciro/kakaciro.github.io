Chanel De Ave: Overview

The De Ave represents Coco's enduring legacy. Featuring diamond-quilted leather and gilded hardware, it embodies the pinnacle of French luxury.

Care and Maintenance for Chanel De Ave

Store your De Ave upright in the dust bag when not in use. Avoid exposure to direct sunlight to preserve the leather suppleness.

Clean with a soft dry cloth monthly. Condition leather every 3-6 months using only approved products to maintain the natural patina.

For hardware oxidation consult a professional. Annual professional cleaning will ensure your De Ave lasts for generations.
