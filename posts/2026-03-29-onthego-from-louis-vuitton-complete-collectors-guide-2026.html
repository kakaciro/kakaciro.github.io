Louis Vuitton Onthego: Overview

The Onthego is a versatile house icon from Louis Vuitton. Monogram canvas construction ensures durability for daily use.

Louis Vuitton Onthego Price Guide

The Louis Vuitton Onthego occupies a distinct position in the luxury bag market:

Retail: USD 2000 to USD 15000, varying by hardware finish and special order.

Secondary Market: Pre-owned examples typically sell for 70-95% of retail depending on condition. Rare special order colors can exceed retail significantly.

Price Factors: Togo leather, rose gold and rare hardware affect final price.
