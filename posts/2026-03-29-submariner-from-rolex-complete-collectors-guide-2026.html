Rolex Submariner: Overview

The Submariner by Rolex is a benchmark in horology. In-house movements and 904L stainless steel ensure decades of reliable service.

How to Authenticate Rolex Submariner

Luxury watch authentication requires checking multiple verification points:

Movement: The Submariner features in-house caliber. lume pips should function precisely.

Case Details: Serial engravings must be deeply engraved. Replicas show shallow engravings.

Documentation: service records should match the serial number on the watch.

Rolex Submariner Price Guide

Retail and secondary market pricing for Rolex Submariner:

Retail: USD 8950 to USD 48000, varying by bracelet.

Secondary: Pre-owned prices range from 80-110% of retail depending on completeness.

Watch Care for Rolex Submariner

Service every 5-10 years to ensure movement accuracy. Avoid magnetic fields and extreme temperatures to maintain timekeeping precision.

Rinse with fresh water after saltwater exposure and dry with a soft cloth. Avoid chemicals and cosmetics to preserve the case finish.
