How to Authenticate Manolo Blahnik Maries

Authenticating a Manolo Blahnik Maries requires examining key details that distinguish originals from replicas:

Key Points: check the craftsmanship, check the material, check the hardware, check the stitching.

Hardware on an authentic Maries shows precise engravings with clean edges. Counterfeits often display inconsistent spacing. Examine the serial placement under good lighting.

The edge finishing is consistent throughout. Authentic pieces use full-grain leather that develops natural character over time.

Care and Maintenance for Manolo Blahnik Maries

Store your Maries upright in the dust bag when not in use. Avoid exposure to direct sunlight to preserve the leather suppleness.

Clean with a soft dry cloth monthly. Condition leather every 3-6 months using only approved products to maintain the natural patina.

For hardware oxidation consult a professional. Annual professional cleaning will ensure your Maries lasts for generations.
