# Cartier Love Collection: Styling Guide

The Cartier Love bracelet is iconic. Here's how to pair it with the right luxury bag.

## The Love Aesthetic

**Minimalist Luxury**
- Clean lines
- Understated elegance
- Symbol of commitment

**Color Palette**
- Rose gold, yellow gold, platinum
- Neutral bag colors work best
- Black and beige complement well

## Bag Pairing by Brand

**Chanel Classic Flap**
- Works with all metal colors
- Beige gives warm, cohesive look
- Black provides contrast

**Hermès Birkin/Kelly**
- Yellow gold for classic combination
- Rose gold for younger aesthetic
- Togo leather shows off bracelet

**Louis Vuitton Neverfull**
- Yellow gold pairs with Monogram
- Avoids looking too casual
- Perfect for daily luxury

## Beyond the Love Bracelet

Cartier also offers:
- **Juste un Clou**: edgier, modern
- **Trinity**: three colors, friendship
- **Panther**: high jewelry, rare
