# Luxury Bag Care & Storage Guide

Whether authentic or premium 1:1 quality, proper care extends bag life dramatically.

## Daily Care

**Rotation**
- Rotate bags regularly to prevent wear patterns
- Never carry the same bag two days in a row
- Stuff loosely when not in use

**Handling**
- Apply perfume before wearing the bag
- Avoid rain and direct sunlight
- Keep away from oily substances

## Storage

**The Right Way**
1. Clean with soft, dry cloth
2. Stuff with acid-free tissue
3. Store in dust bag
4. Keep in original box
5. Maintain 40-60% humidity

**Temperature**
- Never store in extreme heat
- Avoid basements with moisture
- Climate-controlled room is ideal

## Leather Types & Care

**Togo Leather (Hermès)**
- Wipe with damp cloth monthly
- No leather conditioner needed
- Brush gently to maintain grain

**Lambskin (Chanel)**
- Handle with clean hands
- Avoid scratching
- Professional cleaning only

**Canvas (Louis Vuitton)**
- Wipe with damp cloth
- Can use mild soap
- Avoid prolonged sun exposure

**Exotic Leathers**
- Always use professional cleaners
- Condition specifically for exotic
- Store away from other bags

## Hardware Care

**Gold Hardware**
- Polish with jewelry cloth
- Avoid abrasive cleaners
- Store separately to prevent scratching

**Silver Hardware**
- Use silver polish sparingly
- Keep dry to prevent tarnishing
- Store in anti-tarnish bag

## Long-Term Storage Tips

1. **Annual Inspection**: Check for loose stitching, hardware wear
2. **Professional Service**: Clean and condition annually
3. **Authenticity Cards**: Keep in safe location
4. **Photos**: Document for insurance
